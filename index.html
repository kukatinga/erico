About Eric Wambida

A true son of Makongeni, Eric Wambida was born and raised in Kaloleni, Nairobi. As a father of three, family values and community welfare are at the core of his leadership philosophy.

Eric's journey from local schools to becoming a successful tech entrepreneur through his company Scartech Group gives him unique insight into both the challenges facing ordinary residents and the opportunities available through technology and innovation.

His campaign focuses on practical solutions that leverage his dual expertise in technology and community development to create jobs, improve services, and position Makongeni as a model digital ward.

Eric believes in leadership that listens, empowers, and delivers measurable results for all residents regardless of age, gender, or economic status.

Our Vision for Makongeni

Digital Makongeni

Establish ward innovation hubs with free coding classes, digital skills training, and startup incubation spaces to prepare youth for the jobs of tomorrow.

Infrastructure Upgrade

Systematic improvement of roads, drainage, street lighting, and public spaces with transparent tendering processes and community oversight.

Social Welfare

Expand healthcare access, school feeding programs, and support for vulnerable groups through partnerships with NGOs and county government.

Transparent Governance

Quarterly town hall meetings, public budget displays, and a ward app for real-time service delivery tracking and feedback.

Green Spaces

Create urban gardens, tree planting initiatives, and proper waste management systems to improve our environment and public health.

Economic Empowerment

Support SMEs through easier licensing, business training, and access to county procurement opportunities for local enterprises.
